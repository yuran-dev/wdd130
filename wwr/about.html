Our History

Founded in 2010, White Water Adventures started as a small team passionate about rivers and nature. Over the years, we’ve grown into a full-service rafting company offering thrilling adventures for all ages and skill levels. Our journey has been marked by dedication, safety, and unforgettable experiences shared with our amazing clients.
